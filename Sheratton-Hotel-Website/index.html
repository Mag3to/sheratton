Welcome to Sheratton Regency

Sheratton Regency invites you to enjoy a a special lifestyle in an exceptional setting. The ubiquitous ocean stretches as far as the eye can see. The hotel boasts ocean rooms, studio rooms, and suites with a mixture of cultural and elegant contemporary design, all embracing astonishing views of the ocean.

Perched over the sea, guests can relax completely in exterior loungers facing the sunset and drink in the ambiance of the setting and the laidback charms Sheratton Regency has to offer.
